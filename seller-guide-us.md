# Amazon Seller Guide - United States Marketplace

## Marketplace: US
**Region**: North America  
**Currency**: USD

## Getting Started as a US Seller

### Registration Requirements
To sell on Amazon.com, you need:
- Valid US bank account or internationally chargeable credit card
- Tax identification number (SSN or EIN)
- Business address (can be international)
- Phone number for verification

### Seller Types
1. **Individual Seller**: $0.99 per item sold + referral fees
2. **Professional Seller**: $39.99/month + referral fees

## US-Specific Regulations

### Sales Tax Collection
- Required to collect sales tax in states where you have nexus
- Amazon handles tax calculation and remittance in marketplace facilitator states
- Use Amazon Tax Calculation Service for automated compliance

### Product Compliance
- **FDA Requirements**: Food, cosmetics, medical devices
- **FCC Certification**: Electronics and wireless devices
- **CPSC Standards**: Children's products, toys
- **Energy Star**: Energy-efficient products

## Fulfillment Options

### Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) - US
- **Warehouses**: 175+ fulfillment centers across the US
- **Prime Eligibility**: Automatic Prime badge
- **Storage Fees**: 
  - Jan-Sep: $0.87/cubic foot (standard), $0.56/cubic foot (apparel)
  - Oct-Dec: $2.40/cubic foot (standard), $0.56/cubic foot (apparel)
- **Shipping Speed**: 1-2 day Prime shipping

### Fulfillment by Merchant (FBM)
- Self-managed shipping
- Must meet Amazon's performance standards
- Can use Buy Shipping services

## Fee Structure (US)

### Referral Fees
- Most categories: 8-15%
- Electronics: 8%
- Jewelry: 20%
- Amazon Device Accessories: 45%

### FBA Fees (Examples)
- Small standard (10 oz or less): $3.22
- Large standard (1 lb): $4.75
- Large bulky (50 lb): $21.32

## Best Practices for US Market

### Pricing Strategy
- Competitive pricing is crucial due to high marketplace saturation
- Consider Amazon's automated pricing tools
- Watch for Buy Box eligibility (90%+ required for success)

### Product Listings
- High-quality images (minimum 1000x1000 pixels)
- Detailed bullet points (5 recommended)
- Enhanced Brand Content (A+ Content) for registered brands
- Video content increases conversion by 80%

### Customer Service
- Respond to messages within 24 hours
- Maintain Order Defect Rate < 1%
- Keep Pre-fulfillment Cancel Rate < 2.5%
- Late Shipment Rate < 4%

## Marketing Tools (US)

### Sponsored Products
- CPC bidding starting at $0.02
- Average CPC across categories: $0.50-$2.00
- ROI typically 2-5x for optimized campaigns

### Amazon Vine Program
- Available to Brand Registry owners
- Up to 30 free units per ASIN
- Helps generate early reviews

### Lightning Deals
- $300 fee per deal
- Minimum 20% discount required
- Prime Day and Black Friday premium slots available

## Key Considerations

### Competition
- Highly competitive marketplace
- Many established sellers and brands
- Differentiation through branding and quality is essential

### Returns Policy
- 30-day return window standard
- Electronics: Holiday returns extended
- FBA handles returns automatically
- FBM: You manage returns

### Seasonality
- Q4 (Oct-Dec): 40% of annual sales
- Back-to-School (Aug-Sep): Second peak
- Prime Day (July): Major sales event
- Valentine's Day, Mother's Day: Gift category spikes
