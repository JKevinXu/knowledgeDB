# Amazon Seller Guide - China Marketplace

## Marketplace: CN (Amazon.cn)
**Region**: Asia Pacific  
**Currency**: CNY (Chinese Yuan)  
**Language**: Simplified Chinese  
**Marketplace ID**: AAHKV2X7AFYLW

## Getting Started as a CN Seller

### Registration Requirements
To sell on Amazon.cn, you need:
- Chinese business license (营业执照)
- Chinese bank account
- Legal representative ID card
- Business registration address in China
- Chinese phone number

### Seller Types
1. **Individual Seller**: Not available in CN marketplace
2. **Professional Seller**: ¥300/month (waived for first 3 months for new sellers)

## China-Specific Regulations

### Business License Requirements
- Must have valid Chinese business entity
- Business scope must include e-commerce or relevant product categories
- Annual verification required

### Product Compliance
- **CCC Certification** (中国强制认证): Mandatory for many product categories
- **China Food Safety Law**: Imported food products
- **Cosmetics Registration**: NMPA approval required
- **Medical Device Registration**: NMPA certification
- **Customs Documentation**: For imported goods

### Import Requirements
- Import license for certain categories
- Customs clearance documentation
- Quarantine inspection for food, health products
- Origin certificates

## Fulfillment Options

### Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA) - China
- **Warehouses**: 15+ fulfillment centers across major cities
  - Shanghai, Beijing, Guangzhou, Chengdu, Wuhan, Shenyang
- **Shipping Speed**: 
  - Tier 1 cities: Next-day delivery
  - Tier 2-3 cities: 2-3 day delivery
- **Storage Fees**:
  - Standard size: ¥40/cubic meter/month
  - Oversized: ¥100/cubic meter/month

### Cross-Border E-Commerce (CBEC)
- Bonded warehouse model
- Goods stored in bonded zones
- Lower import duties
- Faster customs clearance

## Fee Structure (CN)

### Referral Fees
- Most categories: 5-15%
- Electronics: 8%
- Fashion: 10-15%
- Books: 10%

### FBA Fees (Examples)
- Small standard (< 500g): ¥8
- Standard (500g-1kg): ¥12
- Large (1-3kg): ¥18
- Oversized (> 10kg): ¥45+

## Best Practices for CN Market

### Platform Adaptation
- **Localization**: Use simplified Chinese in all listings
- **Mobile-First**: 80%+ traffic from mobile devices
- **Live Streaming**: Growing trend for product demonstrations
- **Social Commerce**: Integration with WeChat, Weibo

### Pricing Strategy
- Price-sensitive market
- Frequent promotions expected
- Consider 11.11 (Singles' Day) and 6.18 pricing
- Bundle deals popular

### Product Listings (CN Specific)
- High-quality lifestyle images
- Detailed product parameters table
- Size charts for apparel (Chinese sizing)
- Video content highly recommended
- Customer reviews crucial (Chinese consumers trust peer reviews)

### Customer Service
- **Response Time**: Within 12 hours (CN customers expect faster response)
- **WeChat Support**: Many sellers provide WeChat for customer service
- **Language**: Must provide Chinese-language support
- **Return Policy**: 7-day no-reason return required by law

## Popular Product Categories (CN)

1. **Fashion & Apparel**: Fast-moving, trend-driven
2. **Beauty & Personal Care**: High demand for imported brands
3. **Baby Products**: Premium imported baby products popular
4. **Consumer Electronics**: Tech-savvy consumer base
5. **Health & Nutrition**: Supplements, vitamins (imported preferred)

- **Black Friday**: Growing adoption in China

## CN Marketplace Statistics

- **Active Sellers**: 250,000+
- **Prime Members**: 20+ million (growing)
- **Average Order Value**: ¥180
- **Mobile Traffic**: 85%+
- **Cross-border Purchases**: 60% prefer imported products

## Key Considerations

### Competition
- Compete with local platforms (Taobao, JD.com, Pinduoduo)
- Focus on authentic imported products
- Brand authenticity crucial
- Quality and safety certifications important

### Payment Methods
- **Alipay**: Most popular (60%+ of transactions)
- **WeChat Pay**: Second most popular (30%+)
- **China UnionPay**: Credit/debit cards
- **Amazon Pay**: For Prime members

### Logistics Challenges
- Complex customs procedures
- Multi-tiered city delivery
- Holiday season logistics congestion
- Last-mile delivery in rural areas

## Cross-Border Selling to China

### Global Selling Program
- Sell from overseas to Chinese consumers
- No Chinese business license required
- Products shipped from overseas warehouses
- Subject to import duties and taxes

### Required Documents
- Business license from home country
- Product authenticity certificates
- Import permits (category-dependent)
- Insurance documentation

### Customs and Duties
- Individual cross-border limit: ¥5,000 per transaction
- Annual limit: ¥26,000 per person
- Duty rates: 0-50% depending on category
- VAT: 13% standard rate

## Compliance Requirements

### Consumer Protection Law
- 7-day return policy mandatory
- Product quality guarantees
- Accurate product descriptions required
- False advertising penalties

### E-Commerce Law (电子商务法)
- Business license display required
- Tax registration and payment
- Platform responsibility for seller compliance
- User privacy protection

### Data Localization
- Customer data must be stored in China
- Comply with Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL)
- Cross-border data transfer restrictions

## Cultural Considerations

### Lucky Numbers
- 8: Prosperity (prefer prices ending in 8)
- 6: Smooth, favorable (6.18 shopping festival)
- Avoid 4: Associated with death

### Color Preferences
- Red: Luck, prosperity (popular for packaging)
- Gold: Wealth, premium
- White: Avoid for gifts (associated with mourning)

### Gift Giving
- Avoid clocks, umbrellas (negative associations)
- Even numbers preferred (except 4)
- Premium packaging important
